Voices of Wisdom

با یک گل بهار نمیشود

One flower does not bring spring

Explanation: One person or one small change is not enough to create a big transformation. It takes collective effort or multiple factors to make a difference.

آب که یک‌جا بماند، می‌گندد

Still water becomes stale

Explanation: Stagnation leads to decay. Just like water needs to flow, humans need movement, growth, and change to stay alive and productive.

نیت پاک، نتیجه نیک

Pure intention leads to good outcome

Explanation: When your intentions are sincere and honest, the results will eventually turn out good, even if the path is difficult.

دروغ گو حافظه ندارد

A liar has no memory

Explanation: People who lie often forget their lies, which eventually exposes them. Truth is consistent, but lies are hard to maintain.
